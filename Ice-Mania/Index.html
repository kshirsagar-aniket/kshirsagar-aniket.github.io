Our Humble Begining

11-Feb-2001,

From the very beginning, we started our shop Ice Mania In 2001. We served good quality desserts for customers with the less used of artificial flavouring, colours, or preservatives. Over the years, we had created more than one hundred flavours and, at any given time, our parlours stock 21 flavours, 65% of which are fruit-based. Today, Ice-Mania enjoy the highest revenues in the parlour segment compared to competitors, including international giants.
